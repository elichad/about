## WorkflowHub Club

The weekly [WorkflowHub Club](https://s.apache.org/workflowhub-minutes) is chaired by **Frederik Coppens**.

* **Schedule**: Bi-weekly, Wednesday 10:00 BST / 11:00 CEST
* **Agenda / call details**: <https://s.apache.org/workflowhub-minutes>

Anyone can [join the WorkflowHub club](https://github.com/workflowhub-eu/about/issues/1)! Either sign up on GitHub [issue #1](https://github.com/workflowhub-eu/about/issues/1) or join the next call and introduce yourself..
